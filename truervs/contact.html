Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my RV for sale?

Create an account, click on "Sell Your RV," and follow our step-by-step listing process. We'll guide you through adding photos, descriptions, and pricing.

What are your business hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday, 9AM to 6PM EST. Online support is available 24/7 through our chat system.

How do I become a verified dealer?

Visit our Dealer Portal and complete the verification process. We'll review your application and contact you within 2-3 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for our services. Individual RV transactions are handled between buyers and sellers.
