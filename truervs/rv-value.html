Factors That Affect RV Value

Age

Newer RVs typically hold their value better than older models.

Mileage

Lower mileage generally means higher value for motorized RVs.

Condition

Well-maintained RVs with minimal wear and tear command higher prices.

Location

Market demand varies by region, affecting local prices.
